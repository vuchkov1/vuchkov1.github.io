5 THINGS ABOUT ME

Hi there! My name is Martin and I am currently residing in London, UK. I am studying Computer science with Management at King's College London. I was born and raised in Sofia, Bulgaria.

Since I was 4 I have been going out with my bike almost every day. It's my favourite hobby. Most of the time I ride in my local park with my friends. I also go for a ride in the mountains every now and then.

I have had many bikes troughout the years. Even after 2 of them got stollen I didn't give up on this hobby.

Rock climbing is other thing I really love doing. I started working out in early 2012 with my dad and brother. We go the gym between 2 and 5 times a week. This sport helped us strengthen the bond between us and all three of us enjoy doing it.
The picture on the left was taken in August 2014 at my favourite place to climb called Lakatnik.

Winter is one of my favourite seasons of the year not only because it is snowing and everything looks beautiful but also because I go skiing. I learned how to ski when I was in 1st grade of school and have been skiing every winter since then.

My newest hobby is off-roading in the mountain roads of Bulgaria. I started off-roading in June 2016 with my dad and brother. We go to the mountains for 2 days every other week.
